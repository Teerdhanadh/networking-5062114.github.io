Networking
Concepts

Networking is the exchange of information and ideas among people
with a common profession or special interest, usually in an informal
social setting. Networking often begins with a single point
of common ground.Professionals use networking to expand
their circles of acquaintances, find out about job
opportunities in their fields, and increase their
awareness of news and trends in their fields or
the greater world.
